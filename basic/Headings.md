# Headings
To create a heading, add #(1-6) to the heading text.
## Text
``` markdown
# The head 01
## The head 02
### The head 03
#### The head 04
##### The head 05
###### The head 06
```
## Go
> # The head 01
> ## The head 02
> ### The head 03
> #### The head 04
> ##### The head 05
> ###### The head 06
